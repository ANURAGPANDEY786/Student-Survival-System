How It Works

1) The landing page is publicly accessible and introduces the system modules.
2) When a student selects any module, authentication is required (no guest access).
3) Students can submit reviews and report issues inside specific modules such as PG, Food, or Medical.
4) Other verified students can confirm reported issues to increase reliability.
5) The system calculates risk levels and insights based on real-time student activity.
6) Admin can moderate content, remove fake reviews, and resolve reported issues.
